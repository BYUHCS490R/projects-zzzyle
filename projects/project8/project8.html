Spy x Family

This anime was first released in 2022 written by Tatsuya Endo. The manga has 16 volumes and the anime has 3 seasons. The current seasons is ongoing. You can watch this anime in Crunchyroll, Disney, or Hulu.

Plot

Spy x Family follows a spy named Twilight who creates a fake family to get close to a target for his mission. He adopts a young girl and marries a woman to maintain his cover. Together, this unlikely trio navigates their secret lives while unintentionally becoming a real family.

Loid Forger

Loid is the dad and a highly skilled spy. He is on a mission and disguised himself by creating a fake family. He adopted Anya and had Yor as a pretend wife.

Yor Briar

Yor is the mom, she is a clumsy city hall clerk who lives a double life. Unknown to most, she is a secret assasin. She agreed to be Loid's pretend wife to cover up her identity as an assasin.

Anya

Anya is a young orphan Loid adopted. She has a secret ability to read other people's minds. Her powers is unknown to her parents. Though she is aware of her parents' hidden identities, Anya cherishes her family.
