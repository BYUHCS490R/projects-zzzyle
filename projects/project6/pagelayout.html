Seasonal Flowers

Easter Lily

Scientific name: Lilium longiflorum or commmonly known as Easter Lily originated in Taiwan and Japan. These flowers have oval leaves and the vein enters the horizontal direction. These flowers commonly have white petals, but you can also find pale yellow, pink, orange, or red. The flower's season is throughout April to June.

Meadow Rues

Scientific name: Thalictrum (delavayi) or commonly known as Meadow Rues commonly found in temperate regions such as places in China, South Africa, North America, etc. These flowers are small and does not have petals, but they have long stamens. They often thrive in damp and shaded places, there are 22 species found in Northa America.

Plumbago

Belongs to the family of Plumbaginaceae. This flowering plant is a genus of 23 species, native to warm temperate to tropical regions of the world. Plumbago is present in Hawaii, these flowers are found in front of HGB building. These 5 petaled flowers are found in blue, purple, red, or pink.
