Getting Around Switzerland

The best way to get around Switzerland is by using its efficient and extensive public transportation system, primarily the train network, which is known for being fast, reliable, and comfortable. For those planning to use public transport extensively, a Swiss Travel Pass can be cost-effective, covering trains, boats, and public transit in cities, along with discounts on mountain railways and cable cars.

Some of Switzerland's famous attractions include breathtaking sceneries: The Rhine Falls, Jungfraujoch Sphinx Observatory, and the Staubbachfall.
