Food

Switzerland is known for their chocolate and fondue, but here are underrated dishes from Switzerland that locals love.

Basler Läckerli

The BBC Good Food list includes “Bern-style lekerlis biscuits with hazelnuts,” also known as Berner Haselnusslebkuchen (or Leckerli de Berne). While I enjoy Bern's version of leckerli — soft, sweet biscuits with hazelnuts, candied citrus and honey

Bündner Birnbrot

Birnbrot or Birnenbrot (German) translates to “Pear bread.” The Swiss canton of Graubünden is often associated with this bread, but you can find similar bread in other parts of the country.

Gâteau du Vully

A delicious yeasted cake featuring puits d'amour or “wells of love.”
