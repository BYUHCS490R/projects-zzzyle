Engagement 1 - 11/7

  1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do? Submitting a form will be clicking a link and redirecting you to another site. Whatever you put in it will be redirected there.
  2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do? Method is when you can choose GET or POST. POST is changing, adding, updating, and deleting something. GET is just reading something or clicking on links.
  3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"? Get will show the information in the URL but the POST will hide the information in the URL. The data wil still go through the sever, it just won't show the information you're sending.

  1. What are different ways to style your form? You can edit the spacing, align the items, add colors and box shadows, add fields and legends, etc.
  2. How does flex help for both Desktop and Mobile phones? It makes it easier to edit and configure the form in a big and small screen.
