Structural Tags

  1. What goes in the header? The header is where you put the logo or navigation to helpful pages. Some sites also have the search bar in their header.
  2. What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website? It depends how you want your website to look like. You can put before the header, in, or after the header. Depending on preferences and style. Navigation can contain link for another website, but it is usually links to another page within the website.
  3. What goes in Main? The main is the content of the website. Here you find what the website is about, articles, description, photos, or videos are usually added in the main. The body and content of the website.
  4. What goes in the Footer? The footer is the end of the website, helpful links that are connected to the content, copyrights, legal information, cookies, and other helpful information.
